Main Street Ventures introduced Launch It: Cincy, a program aimed at encouraging student-led startups in Cincinnati. As part of this initiative, Cincy Inno selected five entrepreneurs under the age of 25 who have the potential to become the next power players in the city's startup space. One of these entrepreneurs is Pieces' very own Co-Founder & CEO, Tsavo Knott. Pieces is an Over-the-Rhine-based technology startup that has built a productivity platform that enables saving and sharing of file fragments.
