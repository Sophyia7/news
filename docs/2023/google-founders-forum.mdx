# Google Founders Forum - February 28, 2023

<a href="https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/google-founders-forum">Google Founders Forum Article</a>

Tsavo Knott, CEO of Pieces for Developers, was one of the distinguished speakers at the recent Google Chicago Founders Forum. The event brought together top startup founders, VC firms, and Google leaders to learn, network, and drive innovation through technology. The forum aimed to discuss how disruptive technologies can help build a better tomorrow, and Tsavo shared his insights on how Pieces is solving hard problems and changing the way developers work. The event included an industry innovators panel series that highlighted “Game Changers” in the field, emphasizing the importance of innovation and collaboration in driving progress. The event was a great opportunity for Tsavo to share his vision of Pieces and engage with other leaders in the tech industry.
